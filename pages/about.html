How It All Began

Built From a Project and a Shared Vision

In 2021, three university students were assigned to work together on a project. What started as a simple academic task quickly became a meaningful collaboration. By combining their different skills and perspectives, they delivered a high-quality result and built a strong working relationship.

More than just a project, it marked the beginning of a partnership focused on growth, innovation, and future opportunities.
