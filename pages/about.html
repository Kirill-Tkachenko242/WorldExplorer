How It All Began

Born From a Backpack and a Dream

World Explore was founded in 2009 by two friends who quit their jobs, bought one-way tickets, and spent two years travelling through 40 countries. What they found beyond the postcard images were authentic human connections, ancient traditions and transformative moments that no travel brochure could capture.

When they came home, they had a mission: build a travel company that delivers the same depth of experience that changed their lives. Not mass tourism. Not cookie-cutter tours. Real, raw, unforgettable travel.
